Life of Nelson Mandela in brief:

  • 1918: -Born in Mvezo, South Africa
  • 1940: -Joined African National Congress party.
  • 1960: -Went to prison for fight against aparthied.
  • 1989: -Education at University of SouthAfrica.
  • 1990: -Released from prison.
  • 1994: -Became the first black president of South Africa.
  • 1999: -Retired from active politics
  • 2013: -Died at the age of 95 years
